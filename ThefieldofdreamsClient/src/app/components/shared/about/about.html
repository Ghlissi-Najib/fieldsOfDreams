WHO WE ARE

Born from
the island itself

"

CreteVC was born from a belief that true journeys are not defined by distance — but by the feeling of freedom, discovery, and becoming.

The Crete Vacation Channel delivers an engaging overview of everything worth seeing and doing during your stay. As Crete natives, we carry long-standing experience in the island's tourism industry — from restaurants and museums to retail and local culture.

It is an honour to dedicate our work to supporting tourism and connecting travelers with our local community. Above all, it is a privilege to spend our working days taking a staycation in our own backyard.

We hand-select local partners who embody our values of Intentional Simplicity and Human Connection. Every restaurant, boutique, or guide we feature is more than a service — it is a vital chapter in your personal story of Crete.

✨

These are family-run tavernas that have been feeding the village for three generations. Winemakers who still harvest by hand. Guides who grew up in the gorges they now lead you through. Local businesses that exist because of the community — and give back to it every day.
